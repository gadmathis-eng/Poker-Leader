Common questions

How do I join a circle?

Ask the circle creator for their invite code or link, then tap Join with creator invite on the Circles tab.

How do settlements work?

Pot Master calculates who owes whom after final stacks are entered. The app does not move real money — it only shows informational settlement results you can share with your group.

How do I delete my account?

Open You → gear icon → Settings → scroll to Account → Delete account.

You can also email support@potmaster.app to request deletion.

Cloud sync

Sign in with Apple, Google, or email to sync circles across devices. Cloud sync uses Supabase and is optional — the app also works offline on one device.
