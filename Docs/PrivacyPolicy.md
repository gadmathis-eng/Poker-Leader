# Pot Master Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026

Pot Master uses Supabase for authentication and cloud sync. The app helps home poker groups track buy-ins, final stacks, settlements, history, and leaderboards.

## Data We Store

- Account identifier from Supabase Auth (Apple, Google, or email sign-in)
- Display name and player handle
- Circle names, invite codes, members, and per-circle currency
- Session buy-ins, final stacks, settlement payments, and timestamps
- Friend request metadata

## Data We Do Not Store

- Payment card details
- Bank account details
- Precise location
- Contacts from your address book
- Advertising identifiers

## How Data Is Used

Data is used only to provide app functionality: joining circles, syncing sessions across devices, calculating settlements, and showing history or leaderboards.

## Deleting Your Data

You can delete your account in the app:

1. Open **You**
2. Tap the gear icon to open **Settings**
3. Scroll to **Account**
4. Tap **Delete account**

This permanently deletes your Pot Master account, cloud profile, circles you own, and local synced data on the device.

If you cannot access the app, email **support@potmaster.app** and we will delete your account within 30 days.

## Contact

Questions or privacy requests: **support@potmaster.app**
